EXTROVERTS

An extrovert is a person who thrives in social interactions and gains energy from being around others.
  • Energized by social interactions.
  • Outgoing and sociable.
  • Enjoys being around people and making new connections.
  • Expresses thoughts and emotions openly.
  • Prefers group activities and gatherings.
  • Often seen as talkative and enthusiastic.
